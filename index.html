About Me

Hello, I'm Michal Laskowski, an MSc Urban Planning graduate with a passion for front-end development. With a strong background in urban planning and civil engineering, I bring an analytical approach and problem-solving mindset to the world of web development. My professional journey includes valuable experience as a Town Planner in a telecommunications company, where I helped deploy digital connectivity (4G and 5G) across the UK. This work has deepened my understanding of the digital landscape and further fueled my interest in creating seamless online experiences.

Currently, I'm focused on mastering HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create dynamic and user-friendly websites. My goal is to blend my technical skills with my planning expertise to build impactful digital solutions that connect people, just as my previous work connected communities.

Outside of coding, I’m an avid cyclist who enjoys exploring new trails and a seasoned traveler who has explored various parts of Europe and the US. I also have a passion for cooking and hiking, finding creativity and joy in the kitchen and tranquility in nature. These experiences have enriched my communication skills, adaptability, and problem-solving abilities.

I’m excited to combine my technical skills, creativity, and diverse experiences to build innovative digital experiences that make a difference.
